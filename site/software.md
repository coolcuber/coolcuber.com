# Software I recommend

Here are a few common tasks for most students and academics, along with
the programs I recommend for them. All are free (as in speech) unless
stated otherwise. Not all of these are [things I use
daily](https://codeberg.org/coolcuber/coolcuber-nixos){target="\_blank"},
and some aren\'t even my favorite in their categories, but they are
pieces of software that I always recommend highly.\
\
**Calculator/CAS:**
[Sagemath](https://www.sagemath.org/){target="\_blank"}\
- Built on top of Python\
- Good for quick calculations and detailed computations\
- Easy symbolic computation\
- Has convenient representations of most common algebraic structures\
\
**Text editing:** [Vim](https://www.vim.org/){target="\_blank"}\
- Modal text editor\
- Highly customizable\
- Dedicated online community\
- Wildly efficient (once you get used to it)\
\
**Reading PDFs:**
[Zathura](https://pwmt.org/projects/zathura/){target="\_blank"}\
- Lightweight\
- Vim-like commands/navigation\
- Distraction-free experience\
\
**Annotating PDFs (drawing):**
[Xournal++](https://xournalpp.github.io/){target="\_blank"}\
- Very easy to use\
- Provides basic tools to draw, overlay text, or create shapes on a new
or existing PDF\
- Works great in lecture environments\
\
\
Although these aren\'t quite everyday tasks, here are some more
recommendations:\
\
**Operating System:** [OpenSUSE
Leap](https://get.opensuse.org/leap){target="\_blank"}\
- Great for beginners\
- Stable\
- Easy to install and maintain\
- Long term support (at least the next two years for Leap 16.0)\
\
**Tablet OS:** [LineageOS](https://lineageos.org/){target="\_blank"}\
- Easy install\
- Supports lots of devices\
- Great on \"bargain\" devices left behind by time\
- Just works\
\
\
Here are some tablet applications I recommend. They definitely run on
Android devices, and may or may not run on other devices. They can be
found on [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/){target="\_blank"} and some
may be on the regular app stores as well.\
\
**Whiteboard:** [Linwood
Butterfly](https://butterfly.linwood.dev/){target="\_blank"}\
- Little configuration required\
- Dark mode support\
- Works well for quick and for extended thoughts\
- Great for brainstorming/outlining proofs :)\
\
**Note Taking:**
[Saber](https://saber.adil.hanney.org/){target="\_blank"}\
- Easy to use\
- Dark mode support\
- Easy to convert notes to PDF\
